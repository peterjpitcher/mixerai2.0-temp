Why MixerAI Matters More Than Ever

This week, we had an incredibly productive Search Strategy meeting in Edinburgh hosted by 8 Million Stories (special thanks to Jess McCallum for her outstanding leadership!). The insights from this session only reinforce how critical MixerAI is to our future success.

üîç Key Insights from Edinburgh:

The Search Landscape Has Fundamentally Changed:

  • ChatGPT traffic is up 182% year-over-year (5.14B visits!)
  • Only 40% of searches now result in clicks - the era of "zero-click searches" is here
  • TikTok, Amazon, and AI tools are competing for traditional search market share

Content Must Be AI-Ready:

  • Google's AI Overviews are reshaping search results
  • Content needs proper structure for LLMs to understand and surface
  • Product pages need complete nutrition, ingredients, and allergen data
  • FAQ-style content answering conversational queries is essential

Integration is Non-Negotiable:

  • Silos between search, social, and paid must break down
  • Social platforms are becoming search engines (Reddit citations up 436% in ChatGPT!)
  • Cross-channel attribution is more important than ever
